2.33. Exporting a Git Working Tree

Sometimes you may want a copy of your working tree without any of those .git directories, e.g. to create a zipped tarball of your source, or to export to a web server. Instead of making a copy and then deleting all those .git directories manually, TortoiseGit offers the command TortoiseGit → Export....
